About Us

Tavikmart is Nigeria's one-stop online marketplace for everything you need — from electronics and fashion to home essentials, beauty products, and more.

Our Story

Tavikmart was founded with a simple mission: to make online shopping easy, safe, and accessible for every Nigerian. We believe everyone deserves access to quality products at fair prices, delivered straight to their door.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a trusted marketplace with thousands of products across multiple categories, serving customers across Nigeria every day.
