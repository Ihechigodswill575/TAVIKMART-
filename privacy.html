Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Tavikmart collects, uses and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tavikmart, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address and phone number when you create an account
  • Delivery address when you place an order
  • Payment information (processed securely by Paystack — we never store card details)
  • Order history and browsing activity on our platform
  • Profile photo if you choose to upload one

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order confirmation and delivery updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our products and services
  • Personalise your shopping experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase — one of the world's most trusted cloud platforms. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information. Payment data is handled entirely by Paystack and is never stored on our servers.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Delivery partners — to fulfil your orders (name, address, phone only)
  • Paystack — to process your payments securely
  • Law enforcement — if required by Nigerian law

5. Cookies

Tavikmart uses browser storage (localStorage) to remember your cart and wishlist across sessions. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@tavikmart.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Tavikmart after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us.
